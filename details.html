General Info
A team of 1 to 8 players begins in the surgery room, trying to find the exit. Along the way, they must survive through 100 rooms, including 4 puzzle rooms, 2 boss rooms, and a terrifying chase sequence.

Players can collect evidence throughout their run to earn cash, which can be used to buy new abilities and starting items.

Game Type: Co-op Survival Horror / Puzzle Adventure

Player Abilities
Each player has one unique ability, granted through an incomplete surgery performed by Dr. Herbert. The players escaped the surgery room before the operation was finished, so their abilities remain dormant.

To activate an ability temporarily, players must use a special item known as the blue needle. These needles are rare, so itâ€™s wise to save them for tough battles against strong monsters.

Later in the game, players can unlock a powerful super ability that requires the use of a red needle. Additionally, different abilities can be purchased from the shop.
