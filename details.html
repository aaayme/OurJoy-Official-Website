General Info
A team of 1 to 8 players begins in the surgery room, trying to find the exit. Along the way, they must survive through 100 rooms, including 4 puzzle rooms, 2 boss rooms, and a terrifying chase sequence.

Players can collect evidence throughout their run to earn cash, which can be used to buy new abilities and starting items.

Game Type: Co-op Survival Horror / Puzzle Adventure
Room Types
There are 3 types of rooms:
  • Normal Rooms
  • Puzzle Rooms
  • Boss Rooms
Normal Rooms
they are randomly placed between puzzle rooms and boss rooms. Some are unique—such as the starting room. They may be dark or well-lit, and you may encounter common monsters inside.
Puzzle Rooms
Unique rooms where each one contains a different puzzle. Players must solve the puzzle to unlock the door to the next room. Some puzzle rooms may be dark, and monsters might appear to interrupt the solving process.
Boss Rooms
Special rooms featuring unique boss fights. Some boss rooms may also include puzzles that must be solved to defeat the boss or to survive the encounter.
Player Abilities
Each player has one unique ability, granted through an incomplete surgery performed by Dr. Herbert. The players escaped the surgery room before the operation was finished, so their abilities remain dormant.

To activate an ability temporarily, players must use a special item known as the blue needle. These needles are rare, so it’s wise to save them for tough battles against strong monsters.

Later in the game, players can unlock a powerful super ability that requires the use of a red needle. Additionally, different abilities can be purchased from the shop.
