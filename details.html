Room Types
There are 3 types of rooms:
  • Normal Rooms
  • Puzzle Rooms
  • Boss Rooms

Puzzle Rooms
Unique rooms where each one contains a different puzzle. Players must solve the puzzle to unlock the door to the next room. Some puzzle rooms may be dark, and monsters might appear to interrupt the solving process.
Boss Rooms
Special rooms featuring unique boss fights. Some boss rooms may also include puzzles that must be solved to defeat the boss or to survive the encounter.
