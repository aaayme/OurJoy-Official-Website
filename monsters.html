A shadowy ghost appearing in pitch-black rooms. Tries to possess you, causing major damage. Extremely sensitive to light—light up the room to ward it off.

🪓 The Grafted

A fusion of corpses wielding a cursed axe. Multiple arms, brutal melee attacks. Weak to fire. Requires boosts to defeat.

🕷️ Corpseweaver

A spider made of bodies. Incredibly fast and deadly. If it reaches you—game over. Fire is your only hope.

🧟 Zombies

Failed experiments. Slow, weak, and plentiful. Easy to kill alone—but deadly in numbers.

🛡️ Ferrous: The Legendary Knight

A cursed knight, soul overtaken by a Gooler. Nearly invincible—only his back-mounted sword can be struck. Hard to reach, but your only chance.

🧪 Doctor Herbert

The mad surgeon behind your operation. Leads the monsters. Believes you’re an experiment gone rogue—and wants to fix that.
