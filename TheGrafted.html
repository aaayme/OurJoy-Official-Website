This terrifying boss appears in Chapter 2. After escaping underground horrors, players reach the surface and stumble into a misty graveyard. Surrounded by medieval weapons, a strange axe glows among them. Body parts begin to crawl toward it, fusing into a grotesque creature known only as The Grafted.

Phase 1: Assembled Wrath

The Grafted uses 4 primary attacks in this phase:

  • Axe Slash: Dashes toward a player while dragging his axe, then delivers a heavy, sweeping strike.
  • Multi-Hands Slam: Slams the ground repeatedly with all arms, damaging players in a line.
  • Axe Waving: Creates wind-based damage. Two possible moves:
    • Rolls forward, followed by a downward smash.
    • Throws three slicing wind waves from range.
  • Ground Pound: Slams the axe straight down, creating a shockwave in a circular area.

Phase 2: Twisted Fusion

Once his health drops, The Grafted strikes the floor three times, shattering the surface. The arena collapses, sending all players and the boss into an underground fire chamber with 4 fire launchers.

Here, a plant monster fuses with The Grafted, mutating his arms to stretch long distances. He also becomes immune to all direct attacks.

Once defeated, The Grafted bursts into flames, rolling in agony. Suddenly, a colossal Skeleworm erupts from beneath the arena, snatches him mid-burn, and disappears into the depths.
